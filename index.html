BWC FUN DIVE

Welcome to BWC FUN DIVE! We are a passionate diving center dedicated to providing unforgettable snorkeling and diving experiences around Menjangan Island and the beautiful Pemuteran area. Our team of professional guides ensures that each adventure is safe, fun, and tailored to your skill level, whether you are a seasoned diver or trying it for the first time.


At BWC FUN DIVE, we believe in creating memories that last a lifetime. Our tours are designed to showcase the vibrant marine life and stunning underwater landscapes that make this region a top destination for divers from around the world.
